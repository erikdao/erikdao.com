I start this blog with two purposes 1) to document my journey while learning new things; and 2) to share them with others and my future self. The former reason is to make sure that I understand what I've learned, and also to eliminate my scare of writing ;-) The later is to satisfy my personal wish to contribute to the community. This blog is always a work-in-progress as I'm learning and like to learn new things. That's said there might be something you disagree or want to give your comments about on this blog, and you're more than welcome to do so. Let's talk!

A bit more about myself

I'm living in Stockholm, Sweden and doing a master in machine learning at KTH. I'm originally from Vietnam where I completed a bachelor in computer science at HUST. In 2015, I first played with neural networks during my summer internship at National University of Singapore (NUS). I built a ship image classifier in Caffe and published my first paper during the same year. After my bachelor in 2017, I spent 6 months at King Abdullah University of Science and Technology (KAUST) in Saudi Arabia building fish detector (with Faster-RCNN) and doing weakly-supervised human action localization.

If you keep scrolling down, you can find some more random things about me.
