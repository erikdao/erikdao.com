I start this blog with two purposes 1) to document my journey while learning new things, and 2) to share them with others and my future self. The former reason is to make sure that I understand what I've learned, and also to eliminate my scare of writing ;-) And the latter is to satisfy my wish to contribute to the community. I like to learn new things, and as I'm learning, this blog is always a work-in-progress. Louis - The cat

He's forstered by a friend and has been enjoying his new home with another cat named Tei Tei. As of September 2021, Louis is the father of 5 cute kittens.
