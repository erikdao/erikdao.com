I'm originally from Vietnam. Before coming to Sweden, I spent a couple of months doing research internships in machine learning, computer vision at National University of Singapore (Singapore), and King Abdullah University of Science and Technology (Saudi Arabia). After working as a software engineer for more than four years, I was convinced that I want to transition my career into machine learning. As a starting point, I moved to Sweden to join the master's programme in machine learning at KTH.

I start this blog with two purposes 1) to document my journey while learning new things; and 2) to share them with others and my future self. The former reason is to make sure that I understand what I've learned, and also to eliminate my scare of writing ;-) The later is to satisfy my personal wish to contribute to the community.
